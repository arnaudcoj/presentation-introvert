Are you an introvert ?

  • "I wish I was at home, playing video games..."
  • "You are too calm... What's wrong ?"
  • "Get out of your shell !"

What should the others know ?

Introverts don't hate people !

They just don't NEED to talk.

Introverts enjoy doing calm activities.

"I'm not bored, don't you see I am reading a book ?"

Introverts are not shy and can be social.

But they rather be in a bar than on the dance floor.

What should you do ?

Accept yourself

Introversion is not a mental condition.

Tell the world what you want

People are not your ennemies, but they may not know how you work.

Experience

Listening to your inner self is great,

But sometimes, try to open your mind to the world.
